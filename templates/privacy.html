Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 12, 2026

At StealthGuard, we prioritize your privacy and data security above all else. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle the data you process using our steganography tool.

1. Zero Data Retention

StealthGuard is designed with a strict "Zero Data Retention" architecture. When you upload an image or text to our servers for encoding or decoding, the data is stored temporarily in memory or a temporary directory solely for the duration of the cryptographic process. All files are immediately and permanently deleted from our servers the moment the process is complete. We do not log, store, backup, or analyze your passwords, encrypted messages, or images.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require accounts, emails, or personal details to use the StealthGuard tool. The tool operates completely anonymously.

3. Third-Party Services

Our application is hosted on Vercel infrastructure. While we do not log data, Vercel may collect standard anonymous web traffic logs (like IP addresses and user-agent strings) to ensure platform stability and security. Please refer to Vercel's privacy policy for more information regarding their infrastructure.

4. Cryptographic Disclaimer

We use standard, open-source AES-256 encryption. However, you are solely responsible for the strength of the passwords you use. We cannot recover lost passwords or decrypt images if you forget your key.
